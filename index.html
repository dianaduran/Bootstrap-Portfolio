The player will click on the buttons and the page shows grab 10 static, non-animated gif images from the GIPHY API. The user can click one of the GIPHY images and the gif will animated. If the user clicks the gif again, it will be still again. Also you can add others buttons of animals and see cute gifs about them.

You can play the game ->GIPHY.
